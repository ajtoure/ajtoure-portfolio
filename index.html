airhead.

a meditation app that simulates deep breathing

We set out to make an app that assists the user with their meditation practice. The user selects a time and an animation is generated, along with a quote from our firebase database. The animation runs until the time runs out, at which point the app prompts the user to end their meditation or start again.

Hi, I'm A.J.!

Hi, I'm AJ!

Originally from BC, I grew up running around in the forest. This was the first place that I learned the importance that an environment has on outcomes. Before we can make something that results in positive outcomes, we need to build it a functional home to thrive in. This is the same of people, design, and code.

Having lived many lives in many locations, I'm grateful for the perspective that has given me. I care deeply about the way our world is structured, and I'm excited at the thought of using tech to bridge gaps, foster equity and push the limits of social innovation. My arts background means that I'm always thinking about the impact of what I do, and how it holds up under observation. The goal is not for the end result to always beautiful, but rather for it to always be meaningful.

After all, what we choose to put our energy and life into creating is a reflection of who we are.
